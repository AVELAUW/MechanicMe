Background/History



MechanicMe, incorporated in April of 2022, is an online marketplace for auto repair. As we look forward to the future, we plan to launch our prototype by the first quarter of 2023. That said, Warren Brooks, Founder/CEO, created MechanicMe after experiencing difficulty locating and trusting a reliable mechanic after his 2008 Chevrolet Impala transmission blew out. As a result, Warren took his vehicle to Chevrolet and GMC dealerships to service the vehicle. However, both dealers quoted Warren over $3,500+. As a result, he pivoted his objective to seek out a small mechanic shop because $3,500+ was out of his budget. Unfortunately, finding a reliable small mechanic is easier said than done due to the accessibility of locating them. Warren quickly found out the only way to find a credible mechanic is through word of mouth. Also, Warren lived in Aldine, TX, where mechanics in his geographical area were Hispanic. On account of this, there were language barriers in certain situations which made discussing business difficult and left Warren uncomfortable and frustrated. For this reason, Warren created MechanicMe. A platform that connects the community based on commonalities. More importantly, a platform that fights for social equality for underrepresented groups. MechanicMe will act as a social network for the business community and as a match marker for young aspiring technicians of all genders and sexualities in the automotive industry looking to fulfill their dreams and aspirations. With that being said, we are proud to launch a revolutionary platform that will disrupt the market and play a valuable role in the makeover of the automotive industry.
